# Power BI Projects

A collection of interactive Power BI dashboards built to analyse real-world datasets and answer key business questions.

---

## Project 1: Instagram Analytics Dashboard

![Dashboard](([01_Instagram-Analytics/01_dashboard_PBI.png](https://github.com/Vera-Atiogbe/Power-BI-Projects/blob/main/Instagram%20Analytics%20dashboard%20PBI.png)).

### Overview
Analysed 29,999 Instagram posts to answer 5 key social media strategy questions using Power BI.

### Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- DAX Formulas

### Visuals Used
- Slicers
- Treemap
- Area Chart
- Matrix Heatmap
- Stacked Bar Chart

### Key Questions Answered
1. Which post types generate the most engagement?
2. What are the best times to post?
3. How does reach vary across content types?
4. Which hashtag strategies perform best?
5. What drives follower growth?

## Project 2: Data Professional Survey Breakdown

![Dashboard]([02_Survey-Breakdown/survey_dashboard.png](https://github.com/Vera-Atiogbe/Power-BI-Projects/blob/main/Data%20Professional%20Survey.pbix))

### Overview
Analysed survey responses from 630 data professionals globally to uncover salary trends, job satisfaction, and career entry difficulty.

### Tools Used
- Power BI
- Data Transformation (Power Query)
- DAX Formulas

### Key Findings


-630 survey takers across US, India, UK, Canada and other countries
-Data Scientists earn the highest average annual salary at $94k
-Python is the most popular programming language by far
-Data Analysts earn an average of $55k per year
-Average age of the survey takers is 29.87 years
-Work/life balance satisfaction is 5.74 out of 10
-Salary satisfaction is low at just 4.27 out of 10
-Females earn an average salary of $55,191 per year
-Males earn an average salary of $53,457 per year (females earn slightly more than males)
-42.7% found it neither easy nor difficult to break into data 
-24.8% found it difficult to break into data
